What is Sail?

This year, Sail will be a three-day event held virtually in April. It is completely free to attend, and you can participate from your living room! Come learn more about computer science and life at the University of Illinois through classes taught by current students on topics ranging from Machine Learning and Virtual Reality to The Science of Bitcoin and Texas Hold'em Techniques.

Who is Sail for?

High school students like you! If you're interested in computer science, this is a great way to gain more exposure to the field and make some friends along the way! We welcome students of all backgrounds.

When and where is Sail?

Sail will be held virtually this year, to ensure the safety of our students and attendees. The event will take place from Friday, April 2nd to Sunday, April 4th. Stay tuned for more information regarding student registration as we approach April!

What if I'm an admitted student?

Students admitted to the University of Illinois are strongly encouraged to come! You'll get to experience Sail with fellow admitted students, listen to professors and interact more personally with current students. You'll also have the opportunity to participate in information sessions and activities tailored specifically for admitted students like you!

Other questions?

Please don’t hesitate to contact us at cs-sail@lists.cs.illinois.edu. We’ll get back to you as soon as possible!
